DASCHUND

Who are We?

The popular daschund breed known for their short legs and small long stature has numerous names referred to more than their breed name. Daschund's are also known as the weiner dog, badger dog, and sausage dog. They are born as a smooth-haired, wire-haired, and long-haired. Originally the daschund was breed to scent, chase, and flush out badgers and other burrow-dwelling animals.
